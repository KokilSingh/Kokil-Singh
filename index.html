About Me

Presently, I am pursuing double majors: Biological Sciences (M.Sc.) and Computer Science (B.E.). I have a voracious appetite for knowledge; Ever curious about anything and everything that I come across. This has led me to explore the world of research. While I love both my domains, the inter-disciplinary fields of Biological Sciences and Computer Science, viz., Bioinformatics, Computational Biology and Biomedical Engineering interest me the most.
